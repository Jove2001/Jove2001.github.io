    Creators companion

    Summary

    The Rick was inspired by classic arcade cocktail cabinets. I wanted a device that would help me to be more mindful with my technology use... and to help me to take over the world.

    I kept finding myself scrolling mindlessly on my phone at home. Just checking the time lead to me checking my notifications, to checking social media, to endlessly scrolling. I decided that I needed a dedicated lounge room device that could be optimised for a limited number of tasks. It should be:

  • A device to fill in for the now ubiquitous "second-screen" without being a hand-held device.
  • "Invisible". It would look like a normal piece of furniture.
  • A dashboard for the home
  • A 2P retro gaming cabinet
  • A prototyping workstation for the modern creator and be able to create a legion of robots to do your bidding.
  • Somewhere to charge your phone and stuff.

    "Design first, then build"

    I started designing The Rick while I was doing sales in the timber yard at Bunnings. I really liked working there and I tried to source as much material as possible from Bunnings. They had a personal project section in their internal staff application to encourage this sort of project, and it made it really easy to keep track of the materials I needed for the "case".
