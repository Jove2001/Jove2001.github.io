    Creators companion

    Summary

    • Inspired by an arcade cocktail cabinet.
    • A dashboard for the home
    • 2P retro gaming cabinet
    • A prototyping workstation for the modern creator.
    • Create a legion of robots to do your bidding.
    • Charges your phone and stuff.

    Featuring

    • Integrated monitor with 180° viewing angle
    • Home dashboard view
    • Discreet wireless phone charging
    • GPIO and Arduino pin-outs
    • Dual front USB-3 inputs for arcade controllers / charging
    • Fan cooling

    Hardware

    • LattePanda 3 Delta
    • Kew Labs UTS-1T wireless charging

    Software:

    • Custom Ubuntu Cinnamon 22.04
